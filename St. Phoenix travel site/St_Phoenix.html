St. Phoenix

Relax. Explore. Enjoy.

About the island:

Located somewhere in the Caribbean, this small, secluded and little-known island is the perfect place for your private tropical getaway.

We provide our guests with luxury accomodations in a quiet and peaceful setting.

Restricted access to the island means limited crowds and guaranteed solitude.

White sand beaches cover the entire perimeter of the island, surrounding a forest-covered dormant volocano with 360° views from the top.
